# Celiac-Safe Dining Guide — Road Trip Route

**Purpose:** Celiac disease is a serious medical condition, not a preference. This guide distinguishes between:
- **DEDICATED** — 100% gluten-free facility: no wheat/gluten ever in the building. Safest option.
- **CELIAC-AWARE** — Shared kitchen but strong protocols: dedicated fryer(s), separate prep surfaces, trained staff, consistent community praise from celiacs. Lower risk, call ahead.
- **GF-FRIENDLY** — Gluten-free menu items available. Not specifically vetted for celiac safety. Avoid unless noted.

**Before every meal:** Call ahead, mention Celiac disease (not just "gluten-free"), and confirm protocols. Menus and staff change.

## Oregon Coast

### Cannon Beach, OR

#### ★★ PUBLIC COAST BREWING COMPANY — CELIAC-AWARE (Destination Pick)
- **264 3rd St, Cannon Beach, OR**
- The strongest celiac option on the Oregon Coast. Owner's son has celiac disease — this shapes the entire kitchen culture. Nearly every menu item available GF: GF-battered fish and chips, onion rings, burgers on GF buns, clam chowder. Dedicated fryer. Staff changes gloves, uses separate containers, and is deeply trained. Listed in the National Celiac Association restaurant directory.
- **Note:** Not a dedicated facility (they brew gluten-containing beer), but protocols are unusually strong for a pub.
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — this is easily the best Celiac pub meal on the entire Oregon Coast.

#### Pizza A'fetta — CELIAC-AWARE
- Cannon Beach
- Separate kitchen area and separate oven for GF pizza. Call ahead to confirm current protocols.

#### Tom's Fish and Chips — CELIAC-AWARE
- Cannon Beach
- GF grilled fish + fries in a dedicated fryer. Limited menu but solid for a safe coastal fish meal.

- **1436 2nd St, Eureka, CA 95501 | (707) 443-7339**

#### Hey Juan Burritos — CELIAC-AWARE

## Marin County / Sausalito

### Sausalito, CA

#### ★★ COPITA TEQUILERIA Y COMIDA — DEDICATED (Destination Pick)
- **739 Bridgeway, Sausalito, CA 94965 | (415) 331-7400**
- **100% gluten-free kitchen.** No gluten enters the kitchen at all. Created by chef Joanne Weir. Upscale Mexican: tacos, enchiladas, mole, fresh tortillas. Community consensus is that it is fully safe for celiac. Waterfront Sausalito location makes it a natural meal stop when visiting the Bay Area.
- **Caveat:** They serve beer (with gluten) and cannot confirm mixed drinks are safe — the kitchen is GF but the bar is not.
- **Worth a stop:** Absolutely. A genuinely good restaurant that is also a dedicated GF facility — rare combination.

### Marin County (Mill Valley / San Anselmo)

#### ★ FLOUR CRAFT BAKERY — DEDICATED
- **San Anselmo** and **Mill Valley Lumber Yard** locations
- Marin County's premier dedicated GF bakery. Entire facility is gluten-free. All supplier flour and ingredients are certified GF with documented provenance. Artisan pastries, breads, morning pastries, sandwiches, quiche, toast, salads. Run by a gluten-intolerant founder (Heather Hardcastle) who built this as the bakery she needed.
- **Celiac community status:** Featured by the Celiac Community Foundation of Northern California as a "Bite of the Month."
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — stock up on road trip snacks here. Among the best dedicated GF bakeries in California.

## San Francisco, CA

San Francisco is one of the most celiac-friendly cities in the US. Multiple 100% dedicated GF establishments.

#### ★★ PICA PICA AREPA KITCHEN — DEDICATED (Top Pick)
- **401 Valencia St, San Francisco, CA 94103**
- **100% gluten-free** Venezuelan kitchen. Founded 2008, SF location opened 2010. Entire menu built on corn, yuca, and plantains — naturally GF. They test recipes at certified labs (Bia Diagnostica Labs and NIMA) and post results publicly. Partnership with the Celiac Community Foundation of Northern California. Arepas, cachapas (sweet corn pancakes), yuca fries, plantains.
- **Celiac community status:** Official partner of the Celiac Community Foundation. Lab-tested menu.
- **Worth a detour:** Yes. Exceptional food that's also one of the most transparently celiac-safe restaurants on the entire route.

#### ★★ KITAVA — DEDICATED
- **2011 Mission St, San Francisco, CA 94110**
- **100% gluten-free**, also free of dairy, corn, soy, peanuts, refined sugar, and seed oils. Paleo/whole-food approach. Build-your-own bowls: Cuban bowl with plantains, barbacoa power bowl, tuna bowl, GF fries, chicken nuggets, cassava brownies. The fryer is GF. Also has an Oakland location.
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — great for a healthy, satisfying meal with zero anxiety.

#### ★★ MARIPOSA BAKING COMPANY — DEDICATED (Destination Pick)
- **1 Ferry Building, Suite 32, San Francisco, CA 94111**
- **100% gluten-free** artisan bakery at the iconic Ferry Building Marketplace. Breads, pastries, cakes, bagels, cinnamon rolls, chocolate croissants, sandwiches, pasta. Hours: Mon–Fri 9:30am–4pm, Sat 9am–4pm, Sun 10am–4pm.
- **Why it matters:** The Ferry Building is a natural San Francisco stop. Mariposa lets a celiac traveler eat freely alongside non-GF partners who are shopping the market.
- **Worth a detour:** Yes — world-class GF baked goods in a world-class setting.

#### Third Culture Bakery — DEDICATED
- San Francisco
- 100% GF cafe and bakery. Validated as a Gluten-Free Safe Spot by the Gluten Intolerance Group's (GIG) GFFS certification program. Mochi muffins and fusion pastries.

#### Firefly Restaurant — CELIAC-AWARE
- Noe Valley, San Francisco
- Not dedicated, but highly praised in the celiac community for date-night safety. Staff takes cross-contact seriously. Call ahead.

## Monterey / Carmel-by-the-Sea, CA

#### ★ NECE'S GF BAKERY — DEDICATED
- **25 Soledad Dr, Monterey, CA 93940 | (831) 316-9768**
- Monterey County's only dedicated GF bakery. No outside food allowed inside to protect celiac customers. Cinnamon rolls, muffins, cookies, sandwiches, morning buns. Clean, deliberate operation.
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — pick up breakfast or snacks here before heading to the aquarium or Big Sur.

#### Carmel's Bistro Giovanni — CELIAC-AWARE
- **San Carlos between 5th & 6th Ave, Carmel-by-the-Sea, CA**
- Not dedicated, but has the strongest celiac reputation of any full-service restaurant in the Monterey/Carmel area. Staff-wide celiac training: every staff member knows what celiac is. Separate cookware and prep area for GF dishes. GF pasta, GF bread, dessert. Reviewed positively by celiac diners who used gluten-detection devices and had zero reactions.
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — for a proper sit-down dinner in charming Carmel, this is the best option. Call ahead.

#### Manasiri's Crepes & Sandwiches — CELIAC-AWARE
- Monterey area
- Entire menu available as GF crepes. Staff knowledgeable about cross-contact; everything is separated. Good option for a quick, safe lunch.

## Big Sur, CA

Big Sur has **very limited dining options** by design — it's mostly wilderness. The major lodges (Post Ranch Inn, Ventana) have restaurants but are not known for celiac-specific protocols. Options:

- **Self-catering is strongly recommended** for Big Sur nights. Stock up in Carmel before entering.
- For any restaurant stop, call ahead specifically about celiac (not just GF). Kitchen staff at the lodge restaurants tend to be more accommodating to direct requests than their general menus suggest.
- Closest safe option before Big Sur: Carmel's Bistro Giovanni or Nece's GF Bakery (Monterey).

## Santa Barbara, CA

Santa Barbara ranks among the best celiac cities in the US — 8 dedicated GF facilities.

#### ★★ LILAC MONTECITO — DEDICATED (Destination Pick)
- **1209 Coast Village Road, Montecito, CA (near Santa Barbara)**
- **100% dedicated gluten-free fine dining.** California bistro meets European technique. Seasonal rotating menu with signature dishes including buffalo cauliflower, burrata salad, chicken marsala, fettuccine alfredo, Mediterranean risotto. Chic black-and-white interior, European patio on Coast Village Road.
- **Why it matters:** Fully dedicated GF facility at fine-dining quality is exceptionally rare. This is the kind of restaurant where a celiac diner can read the entire menu and order anything without asking a single question.
- **Worth a detour:** Yes — this is a genuine destination restaurant. One of the best celiac dining experiences on the entire route.
- Hours: Breakfast/lunch daily; dinner Tuesday–Sunday.

#### ★ LILAC PATISSERIE — DEDICATED
- Santa Barbara, CA
- Same team as Lilac Montecito. 100% dedicated GF bakery. Breads, cakes, croissants, sandwiches, pastries.
- **Worth a stop:** Yes — pick up pastries or a GF baguette for the road.

#### ELUBIA'S KITCHEN — DEDICATED
- **Isla Vista location** (near Santa Barbara/Goleta) — grand opening April 2025
- Dedicated GF Latin American: pupusas, street tacos, burritos, tamales, garnachas, enchiladas, bowls. Founded by Elubia Orozco after her own GF health journey. Everything GF, with a good vegan selection.
- Customers report being able to "look at the whole menu and order anything" — the hallmark of a dedicated facility.
- **Note:** Goleta food truck location shows as temporarily closed on Yelp (Dec 2025). Confirm Isla Vista location status before visiting.

## Los Angeles Area (Malibu / Santa Monica / Venice / West Hollywood)

Los Angeles has one of the most robust dedicated GF dining scenes in the country.

#### ★★ SHOJIN — DEDICATED (Top Destination Pick)
- **Culver City location** (primary recommendation for this route)
- Also: Little Tokyo, downtown LA (verify open status — downtown location showed closed on Yelp)
- **100% gluten-free AND vegan** Japanese restaurant. Started specifically to serve people with multiple severe allergies (celiac, soy, nut, sesame, onion, garlic). Entire menu clearly labeled for every major allergen. Macrobiotic, organic, no artificial ingredients, no microwaves. Dishes include sushi, ramen, tempura-style items, rolls — all GF.
- **Why it matters:** The novelty of eating sushi, ramen, and Japanese food safely — without worrying about soy sauce, tempura batter, or cross-contact — is genuinely remarkable for a celiac diner.
- **Worth a detour:** Yes, strongly. One of the most unique dedicated GF restaurants in the US.

#### ★ BREADBLOK — DEDICATED BAKERY
- **Silver Lake: 1529 Griffith Park Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90026** (verify open — Santa Monica location closed Oct 2025)
- 100% GF artisan bakery: organic, free of gums, soy, and refined sugars. Croissants, sourdough, sandwich bread, pastries.
- **Note:** Confirm the Silver Lake location is currently open before planning around it.

#### Venice Dedicated Options (401 Lincoln Blvd area) — DEDICATED
- At least one dedicated GF restaurant at 401 Lincoln Blvd, Venice, CA. Staff check spices and ingredients from 100% GF suppliers. Confirm current name and hours via FindMeGlutenFree before visiting, as this area sees restaurant turnover.

#### True Food Kitchen — CELIAC-AWARE (multiple LA locations)
- Santa Monica and other LA locations
- Not dedicated, but consistently praised for celiac protocols. Menu clearly marks celiac-friendly items. Servers trained on celiac vs. preference distinction. Good for a reliable, upscale-casual safe meal.

#### The Misfits (Santa Monica) — CELIAC-AWARE
- Santa Monica, CA
- Dedicated GF fryers. GF Green Chile Mac & Cheese highlighted. Community positive reviews from celiacs.

#### Moody Rooster (Malibu) — CELIAC-AWARE
- Malibu, CA
- High-end food; multiple celiacs report eating there safely. Good option along the Malibu coastal drive. Call ahead.
